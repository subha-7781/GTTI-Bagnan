Air-Conditioning and Refrigeration Department

What is an AC Repairing Course?

HVAC or AC Repairing courses teach students how to become professionals who can repair heating and cooling appliances. You get to learn how the issues can be fixed in an AC repair institute. In-depth technical training is given to students regarding the same at George Telegraph Training Institute (GTTI) AC repairing training institute in Kolkata.

What do you learn in this course?

This is a 360-degree program covering all kinds of repairing and servicing elements of AC repairing and servicing. Students also learn about the various different brands of HVAC components. They learn how to work around them differently, Amateur students flourish as talented AC repair professionals by the end of these courses.

HVAC Course Elements:

  • Compressor (Hermetic, Semi Hermetic, Open Type)
  • Condenser (Air Cooled/Water Cooled)
  • Evaporator
  • Expansion Device
  • Thermometer
  • Gauges (Compound & Pressure)

Use of Measuring Instruments:

  • Voltmeter
  • Ammeter
  • Ohmmeter
  • Multimeter
  • Series and Parallel Connection
  • Anemometer

Scope of HVAC Courses

Working knowledge of HVAC is a highly prized ability, regardless of whether one wants to specialize in heating, air conditioning, or all systems. HVAC specialists will always be required because homes and businesses depend on environmental control. That is why the AC repairing course is so popular in Kolkata.

Modules of HVAC Training

Air-Conditioning & Refrigeration Engineering

Eligibility: Passed Class X Board Exam
Duration: 24 months
Course Outline: Trade Theory, Trade Practical, Engineering Drawing, Workshop Calculation & Science, Washing Machine Technology, Workshop Technology, Foremanship.
Work Area: As service and maintenance engineers, supervisors, field supervisors, sales representatives of all types of Air-conditioning & Refrigeration systems and washing machines.

Air-Conditioning & Refrigeration Technician

Eligibility: Passed Class IX
Duration: 12 months
Course Outline: Trade Theory, Trade Practical, Engineering Drawing, Workshop Calculation & Science, Workshop Technology, Troubleshooting.
Work Area: As service and maintenance engineers, supervisors, field supervisors, sales representatives of all types of Air-conditioning & Refrigeration systems.

Air-Conditioning & Refrigeration Mechanic

Eligibility: Passed Class VIII
Duration: 06 months
Course Outline: Trade Theory, Trade Practical, Engineering Drawing, Workshop Calculation & Science, Workshop Technology.
Work Area: As service and maintenance engineers of all types of Air-conditioning & Refrigeration systems.
